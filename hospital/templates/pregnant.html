Pregnant Women

During pregnancy, it's crucial for women to maintain a balanced and nutritious diet to support both their health and the development of the baby.

Focus on a diet rich in:

  • Fruits and vegetables for vitamins and minerals
  • Whole grains for fiber and energy
  • Lean proteins for essential amino acids
  • Dairy or dairy alternatives for calcium
  • Iron-rich foods to prevent anemia

Consult with a healthcare professional for personalized nutritional advice during this crucial time.
